God of War: Chains of Olympus

Kratos, still serving the gods, must fight to save the land from a growing darkness. The story takes place before the events of the first God of War, with Kratos as a champion of the gods, battling mythological creatures.

God of War: Ghost of Sparta

Kratos, the Spartan warrior haunted by the visions of his mortal past, embarks on a journey to understand his origins. He seeks answers about his mother, Callisto, and the existence of his brother, Deimos. The game is a prequel to the main series, exploring Kratos' early life and the events that led him to become the "Ghost of Sparta".

God of War

Kratos, a Spartan warrior who is tasked by the gods to kill Ares, the god of war. Kratos, however, is also haunted by his past, as Ares had tricked him into killing his family. To fulfill his quest, Kratos seeks out Pandora's Box, the only object capable of defeating Ares.

God of War: Betrayal

Kratos leads his Spartan army on a campaign of revenge across Greece after defeating Ares. During his campaign, Kratos is attacked by creatures led by Argos, a giant dog sent by the gods to stop him. After a series of battles, Argos is killed by a mysterious assassin, who blames Kratos for trying to incite the gods against him.
